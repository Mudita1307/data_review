# Suraksha Lens

### Climate Exploitation Risk Intelligence Platform for South Asia

A flagship initiative of the South Asian Institute of Crime and Justice Studies (SAICJS).

---

## About

Suraksha Lens is an AI-enabled early warning and early action platform designed to identify and prevent climate-linked exploitation before it occurs.

The platform combines climate intelligence, geospatial analytics, socio-economic vulnerability indicators, and digital risk monitoring to surface early indicators of trafficking, unsafe migration, labour exploitation, and emerging scam risks in climate-affected regions.

At the core of the system is the **Climate Exploitation Risk Index (CERI)** — a composite framework that models how climate hazards interact with exposure and structural vulnerabilities to increase exploitation risk across geographies.

Suraksha Lens is designed to support:
- Governments
- Civil society organisations
- Humanitarian actors
- Researchers
- Community networks
- Disaster response stakeholders

through anticipatory, evidence-based protection and coordinated response systems.

# Climate Exploitation Risk Index (CERI)

The Climate Exploitation Risk Index (CERI) adapts the disaster risk assessment framework of the United Nations Sendai Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction (2015–2030) to analyse second-order social harms emerging from climate stress.

CERI evaluates risk through three interconnected dimensions:

## Tier 1 — Hazard

Climate hazard intensity and frequency:
- Floods
- Cyclones
- Droughts
- Heatwaves
- Extreme weather events

## Tier 2 — Exposure

Population and livelihood exposure within climate-stressed geographies.

## Tier 3 — Vulnerability

Underlying socio-economic and governance conditions influencing exploitation risk, including:
- Poverty
- Informal labour
- Migration dependence
- Gender inequality
- Marginalisation
- Weak institutional systems

The final index operationalises exploitation risk through the interaction of hazard, exposure, and vulnerability, enabling spatial risk assessment across districts and regions.

# Why It Matters

Climate change is increasingly driving displacement, livelihood disruption, and unsafe migration across South Asia. These disruptions often heighten the risk of exploitation, trafficking, and digital harm, particularly for already vulnerable populations.

Suraksha Lens aims to bridge the gap between climate risk intelligence and human protection by connecting data, technology, research, and community insight into a unified anticipatory protection system.
